Malabar Hill Nature Trail 09 mins

The Malabar Hill Elevated Nature Trail is Mumbai's first elevated forest walkway. This 485-meter wooden canopy walk winds through a dense 12-acre urban forest near Hanging Garden, offering birdwatchers a quiet escape and panoramic views of Girgaon Chowpatty and the city skyline.
