Bandstand Promenade 09 mins

Stretching along Bandra's scenic coastline, Bandra Bandstand is one of Mumbai's most iconic waterfront promenades. Framed by sea views, heritage landmarks, and lush greenery, it offers a rare sense of openness within the city. From leisurely morning walks to sunsets over the Arabian Sea, Bandstand remains a cherished destination that embodies Bandra's relaxed yet distinguished character.

Malabar Hill Nature Trail 2 09 mins

The Malabar Hill Elevated Nature Trail is Mumbai's first elevated forest walkway. This 485-meter wooden canopy walk winds through a dense 12-acre urban forest near Hanging Garden, offering birdwatchers a quiet escape and panoramic views of Girgaon Chowpatty and the city skyline.
